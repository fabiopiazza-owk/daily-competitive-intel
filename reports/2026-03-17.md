## 3. Technical Frontier

- **CellVoyager (Nature Methods):** Autonomous scRNA-seq agent that generates novel biological hypotheses. Architecturally relevant — uses iterative tool-calling loops with statistical validation. K Platform's biomarker module should benchmark against this on standard discovery tasks. [Paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-026-03029-6)
- **ScienceClaw + Infinite (HuggingFace):** Multi-agent framework for autonomous scientific investigation with decentralized artifact exchange. Relevant design pattern for K Platform's multi-modal pipeline orchestration — agents independently analyze pathology/genomics/clinical data and share intermediate artifacts. [Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.14312)
- **Mixture-of-Depths Attention (Arxiv):** Addresses signal degradation in deep transformers by allowing layers to selectively attend to earlier features. Directly applicable to our pathology foundation models where deeper ViTs suffer from feature dilution on whole-slide images. [Paper](http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15619v1)
- **sebis @ ArchEHR-QA: Local EHR QA on a single notebook** — demonstrates competitive clinical QA with small local models, relevant for our on-premise deployment requirements where pharma clients restrict data egress. [Paper](https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.13962)

## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### **Rank 1: Customer-Managed GPU Deployment Mode**
**Rationale:** The Roche-NVIDIA deal signals that Tier-1 pharma is building its own AI compute infrastructure. K Platform must support deployment on customer-owned GPU clusters (NVIDIA DGX/HGX) without requiring Owkin-hosted infrastructure. This is becoming a procurement gating criterion — AstraZeneca and Sanofi likely have similar internal GPU scaling plans. Build a "bring your own compute" mode with Kubernetes-based orchestration on customer infrastructure.

### **Rank 2: Autonomous Biomarker Discovery Agent**
**Rationale:** CellVoyager's Nature Methods publication and the ScienceClaw multi-agent framework validate that autonomous biological discovery agents are reaching production quality. K Platform should ship an agent-based biomarker discovery workflow that (a) autonomously explores multimodal datasets (pathology + genomics + clinical), (b) generates ranked hypotheses with statistical evidence, and (c) produces GxP-compliant audit trails that open-source agents cannot. This is our differentiation moat vs. academic tools. Target: working prototype for Q3 internal demo.

### **Rank 3: Explainability & Clinician-in-the-Loop Dashboard for Trial Sites**
**Rationale:** The AMA survey (81% adoption, 88% skill-erosion fear, 50% oppose AI pathology interpretation) combined with HHS's governance push means our pharma clients' trial investigators will demand clear explainability for any K Platform output that influences clinical decisions. Build a dedicated trial-site-facing dashboard showing model rationale, confidence intervals, and mandatory clinician sign-off gates. This is both a regulatory de-risking feature and a competitive differentiator vs. black-box competitors.
