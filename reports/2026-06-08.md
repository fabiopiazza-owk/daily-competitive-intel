## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### 🥇 Hierarchical graph memory for whole-slide pathology agents
**Rationale:** MemDreamer's decoupled perception/reasoning architecture maps almost directly onto K's WSI problem — token explosion and attention dilution are identical pain points. A graph-memory + agentic retrieval prototype on a single TCGA cohort could demonstrate 3–5× inference cost reduction and enable hours-of-context reasoning over patient pathology archives. Differentiates against Phylo/Owkin-incumbents on multi-slide longitudinal reasoning.
**Effort:** 6–8 weeks for prototype on one indication; 1 research engineer + 1 ML engineer.
