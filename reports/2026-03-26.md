## 3. Technical Frontier

- **MARCH: Multi-Agent Reinforced Self-Check for LLM Hallucination** — Proposes a multi-agent RL framework where agents cross-verify RAG outputs to detect hallucinations. Directly relevant to K Platform's regulatory-document and literature-based features where hallucination is a GxP compliance risk. The multi-agent self-check pattern could be integrated into our RAG pipelines for clinical evidence synthesis. [Arxiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24579v1)

Critical finding for K Platform teams building regulatory intelligence or protocol analysis features: better retrieval ≠ better answers. Relevant for K Platform's drug discovery module if we extend into target characterization — could dramatically speed up protein family analysis in target prioritization workflows. A **cautionary signal** for all K Platform bio-foundation models: we must validate generalization rigorously and avoid inflated benchmark claims in customer-facing materials.
