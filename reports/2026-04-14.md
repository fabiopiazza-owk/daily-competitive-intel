- **[Multimodal fusion for mCRC prognosis](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41974756/):** ML pipeline fusing imaging + molecular + clinical features for bevacizumab response. Directly comparable to K Platform's MOSAIC-type workflows. Engineering should review fusion methodology and late-vs-early integration choices.

Relevant to K's copilot — could reduce dependency on OpenAI/Anthropic APIs for clinical reasoning features and improve on-prem deployment stories for regulated environments.

While radiotherapy is adjacent (not core K), the uncertainty quantification framework is directly applicable to K's pathology segmentation models where confidence scoring is a GxP requirement.
- **[Neural fields for adaptive optics in two-photon microscopy](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-026-03054-5)** (Nature Methods): Novel computational imaging technique for correcting optical aberrations in living tissue. Relevant to upstream pathology data quality — if adopted broadly, this improves the quality of spatial biology inputs K Platform ingests.
