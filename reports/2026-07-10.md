**Rationale:** With ChatGPT Work now offering hours-long autonomous agents and the Mayo AI-retaliation suit going public, pharma legal/QA will demand tamper-evident trajectory logs, human-in-the-loop checkpoints, model-change control, and dissent-escalation paths *as a baseline*. Ship this and it becomes a defensible moat vs. horizontal agents.
**Effort:** Medium — 1 quarter, cross-team (platform + compliance).

### 🥈 Multimodal Biomarker Model Card v2 with MRMC-style Validation Kit
**Rationale:** The neuroblastoma VLM paper and MULLET liver MRMC trial are the emerging evidentiary bar. Pharma partners will benchmark K models against these designs. A packaged validation kit (protocol templates, reader-study tooling, statistical scaffolding) accelerates every enterprise deal.
**Effort:** Medium — 6–8 weeks, clinical science + product.

### 🥉 DrugGen-2 / Disease-Aware Generative Chemistry Integration
**Rationale:** Rather than compete head-on with open disease-aware generative models, wrap DrugGen-2 (and successors) as a pluggable node inside K's target-to-lead workflow with proprietary multimodal disease-context grounding from Owkin's pathology/genomics data. Turns a commoditization threat into a moat.
**Effort:** Small–Medium — 4–6 weeks prototype, then partner validation.
