## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### 🥇 Real-time clinical trial endpoint streaming + FDA-aligned monitoring dashboard
**Rationale:** The FDA commissioner's "endpoints in the cloud as they occur" statement is a clear regulatory signal. First-mover advantage on a K module that streams trial endpoints with audit trails, alerting, and regulator-shareable views could be decisive in AstraZeneca/Sanofi expansion conversations and unlock a new category vs. Benchling.
**Effort:** Large (1–2 quarters; needs streaming infra, GxP-validated audit trails, regulator UX).

### 🥈 Pharma-grade compliance posture: FedRAMP-equivalent + HITRUST + HIPAA BAA matrix
**Rationale:** OpenAI hitting FedRAMP Moderate while landing on AWS resets the procurement bar. K needs a published, customer-facing compliance matrix and a FedRAMP-equivalent (or actual) roadmap to prevent procurement teams from defaulting to "just use OpenAI on AWS." Direct revenue protection.
**Effort:** Medium (cross-functional; mostly documentation, control mapping, and select audits — 1 quarter).

### 🥉 Case-rubric-based clinical evaluation framework for K's models
**Rationale:** The arXiv paper on case-specific rubrics validated across 823 encounters provides a ready methodology for cost-efficient, clinically valid model evaluation. Adopting this gives K a defensible, publishable validation story for FDA/EMA submissions and pharma model-risk reviews — a moat against generic LLM vendors who can't show clinical eval rigor.
**Effort:** Small–medium (2–6 weeks for methodology adoption; ongoing for benchmark expansion).
