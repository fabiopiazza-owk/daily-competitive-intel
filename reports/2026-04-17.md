Every AZ and Sanofi stakeholder will ask about this within days.
**Effort:** 2-week sprint (ML benchmarking + sales enablement + positioning doc)

### 🥈 Mechanistic Reasoning Layer for Biomarker Reports
**Rationale:** The "Virtual Cells" paper and the broader trend toward explainable biology demand that K Platform move beyond "here's a biomarker signature" to "here's the mechanistic hypothesis for why this biomarker matters." Integrating pathway-level causal reasoning into biomarker discovery outputs would be a major differentiator vs. GPT-Rosalind (which will lack proprietary patient data grounding) and vs. Benchling (which lacks AI reasoning entirely).
**Effort:** 4-6 week design + prototype; leverage existing pathway databases (Reactome, KEGG) + LLM reasoning chain

### 🥉 Agent-Runtime Abstraction Layer (MCP + OpenAI Agents SDK)
**Rationale:** With both Anthropic (MCP) and OpenAI (Agents SDK) shipping competing agent harnesses, K Platform risks being locked into one ecosystem. Building a thin abstraction layer that lets K's agentic workflows (data retrieval, ELN integration, model orchestration) run on either runtime protects optionality and de-risks enterprise deals where customers may mandate one provider.
**Effort:** 3-week engineering spike; define interface contract, implement adapters for MCP and OpenAI harness
