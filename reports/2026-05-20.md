### 🥈 Evidence-seeking clinical agent (ClinSeekAgent pattern)
**Rationale:** The ClinSeekAgent paper crystallizes a capability gap in K's current clinical-reasoning stack — most of our agents assume curated context. Building an iterative, multimodal evidence-seeking agent (literature + EHR + trial registries + internal data) is a defensible differentiator vs. Co-Scientist, and pairs naturally with biomarker workflows.
**Effort:** Medium (1 quarter for v1 on a single use case, e.g., rare-disease cohort identification)

### 🥉 AI output provenance & audit trail layer
**Rationale:** Combines two pressures — FDA uncertainty driving demand for auditability, and OpenAI's provenance push setting expectations. K should ship per-output lineage (model version, data sources, prompts, tool calls, reviewer sign-off) as a first-class object in every workflow, with exportable evidence packs for regulatory submissions.
**Effort:** Medium (8–10 weeks; mostly platform plumbing + UX, leverages existing model registry)
