## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### 🥇 Trajectory-level agent auditing for biomarker discovery workflows
**Rationale:** Two converging signals (Agentic CLEAR + Beyond Final Answers) show evaluation is moving from outputs to reasoning traces. Pharma QA and GxP auditability will demand this; it's also a clear differentiator vs. Biomni's opaque agent loops. Ties directly into AZ/Sanofi audit requirements.
**Effort:** Medium (1 quarter) — instrument existing agents with trace logging + build evaluator harness on top of internal benchmark.

### 🥈 Patient & sponsor trust dossier per K deployment
**Rationale:** Stanford's patient-panel model will cascade to pharma sponsors via provider partnerships. Productizing a per-model "trust card" (training data provenance, external validation results, patient-facing explanation, MAUDE-style monitoring plan) preempts procurement friction and aligns with EU AI Act high-risk documentation.
**Effort:** Medium — leverage existing model cards + add patient-facing layer + MAUDE adverse-event hook.

### 🥉 Benchmark K multimodal embeddings against Gemini Embedding 2 on biomedical tasks
**Rationale:** Gemini-E2 directly threatens our representation moat. We need a public-or-internal benchmark on pathology + omics + clinical text fusion tasks showing K's biomedical specialization outperforms general multimodal models — critical narrative for the next investor and customer cycle.
**Effort:** Small-to-medium (4–6 weeks) — assemble TCGA/CPTAC + clinical text eval suite, run head-to-head, publish blog + internal sales asset.
