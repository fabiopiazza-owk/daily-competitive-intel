Integrate structured oncology KG + multimodal patient embeddings into the trial optimization module.

### 🥈 Organoid & 3D-Culture Image Analysis Module
**Rationale:** The [Leigh syndrome organoid screening paper](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42009687/) and growing pharma investment in complex in-vitro models mean K Platform's current pathology AI (focused on tissue slides) must extend to organoid phenotypic readouts. AstraZeneca's discovery teams are already running organoid screens — being the platform they analyze them on is high-value.
**Effort:** Medium (6–8 weeks, extend existing vision backbone + new annotation schemas)

### 🥉 Long-Context Agentic Workflow with Information Density Optimization
**Rationale:** Multiple papers this week ([GenericAgent](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17091), [EvoMaster](https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.17406)) highlight that context management is the bottleneck for scientific AI agents. K Platform's multi-step pipelines (pathology → genomics → clinical) generate massive intermediate context. Implementing token-efficient context compression would reduce latency, cost, and hallucination risk in our agentic copilot features.
**Effort:** Medium (4–6 weeks, architecture-level change to agent orchestration layer)
