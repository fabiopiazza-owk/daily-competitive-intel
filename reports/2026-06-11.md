## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### 🥇 Pathology Evaluation Harness Standardization
**Rationale:** The arxiv 12407 paper exposes that pathology LLM benchmarks are dominated by trivial design choices (patch size, magnification, aggregation). K's competitive narrative against general-purpose LLMs depends on rigorous, reproducible eval — this is a credibility moat with pharma scientific buyers and regulators.
**Effort:** Medium (4–6 weeks; one ML eng + one pathology scientist).

### 🥈 Single-Cell Data Diversity Audit & Strategy Pivot
**Rationale:** Nature Methods evidence that scale alone yields little gain reframes K's biomarker FM roadmap. Audit current pretraining corpus for tissue/disease/platform diversity and reallocate budget from raw cell acquisition to curated diversity and task-specific adapters. Defensive against Phylo's scale narrative.
**Effort:** Small-Medium (3 weeks audit + ongoing data strategy revision).

### 🥉 EU AI Act Provenance & Output Lineage Module
**Rationale:** With OpenAI signing the EU Code of Practice and EU enforcement accelerating, K Platform needs end-to-end model+data lineage and content provenance tagging on generated clinical reports/biomarker outputs. Required for Sanofi/AstraZeneca EU procurement and a defensible differentiator over US-centric platforms.
**Effort:** Medium-Large (8–10 weeks; cross-team: platform, security, regulatory).
