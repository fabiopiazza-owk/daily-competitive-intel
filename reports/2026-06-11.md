## 1. Top 3 Signals Today

### 🔴 Pathology LLM benchmarking paper exposes WSI evaluation pitfalls
A new study shows that "seemingly inconsequential" design choices (patch sampling, magnification, aggregation) dominate apparent performance differences when general-purpose LLMs are used as pathology baselines on whole-slide images. This directly affects how K Platform's pathology models are benchmarked against GPT-class baselines in customer pilots.
- **K implication:** We need to publish a rigorous, reproducible WSI-evaluation protocol so AstraZeneca/Sanofi pilots aren't gamed by sloppy LLM-baseline comparisons.
- [arxiv.org/abs/2606.12407](http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12407v1)

Bipartisan Policy Center data show AI use surging across all HHS agencies, with FDA leading at +148%. This signals an inflection in regulator readiness — both for AI-enabled submissions and for AI tooling inside FDA review itself.
