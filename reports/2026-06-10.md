**Rationale:** Agentic biology benchmarks are crystallizing as the compliance gate for pharma procurement and FDA AI/ML oversight. Shipping an internal ABC-Bench-style evaluation with published results positions K as the trustworthy enterprise choice vs. open-weight competitors like Gemma 4 and Keye-VL.

### 🥈 Multimodal phase-diagram-guided fusion playbook
**Rationale:** The "When to Align, When to Predict" results give us a principled framework to choose CA vs. CP per K use case (pathology+genomics, pathology+clinical, etc.). Codifying this into our model dev playbook reduces wasted training cycles and gives field engineers a deterministic decision tree.
**Effort:** Small (2–3 weeks; internal whitepaper + reference configs)

**Rationale:** Two Nature Methods papers today converge on the conclusion that *diversity beats size* for single-cell foundation models. Re-architecting K's genomics ingestion to optimize cohort/indication diversity over raw cell count is a defensible technical differentiator and matches enterprise narrative against brute-force competitors.
**Effort:** Medium (5–7 weeks; data pipeline refactor + diversity sampling layer + retraining one reference model)
