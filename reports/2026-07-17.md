## 3. Technical Frontier

- **[AutoSynthesis](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15247v1)** — multi-agent meta-analysis; directly applicable to K's evidence generation for biomarker hypotheses and trial design.
- **[GRASP: Granularity-Aware Search for Agentic RAG](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.10463)** — smarter retrieval routing (when/what/how to retrieve) — highly relevant for K's multi-modal retrieval over pathology + omics + clinical corpora.
- **[Bridge Evidence: static retrieval utility ≠ causal utility](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15253v1)** — argues current RAG evals are misleading for multi-step agents; K needs to redesign internal RAG benchmarks accordingly.
- **[Prompt-guided foundation model tuning for pathology classification](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42462643/)** and **[unsupervised histopathology artifact detection/restoration](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42462642/)** — direct feeds into K's pathology foundation model roadmap (MOSAIC / H-optimus successors).

- **[ADAR-based DNA base editing](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41587-026-03228-8)** — expands target space K's biomarker/target ID workflows should annotate.
