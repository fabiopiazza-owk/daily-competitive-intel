**Effort:** Medium (8–12 weeks).

**Rationale:** As K's agentic workflows expand into multi-step trial design and biomarker discovery, single-step hallucinations cascade. Implementing an online failure-prediction monitor on agent trajectories would meaningfully reduce wasted compute and improve trust signals in customer-facing dashboards.
**Effort:** Medium-high (10–14 weeks) — requires instrumented agent runtime and labeled failure data.
