**Rationale:** With FDA clearing an LLM SaMD *and* a former regulator publicly saying pharma is over-conservative, there is a narrow window to arm AstraZeneca/Sanofi champions with pre-built validation templates, audit trails, and interface-vs-decision-maker documentation aligned to current FDA AI guidance. ### 🥉 Integrate atomic fact-checking (AtomiMed-style) into K's generated clinical/biomarker narratives
**Rationale:** As K's outputs increasingly feed regulated decisions, hallucination in generated reports is our largest downside risk. AtomiMed-style hierarchical fact-checking provides an auditable, per-claim provenance layer that both improves quality and becomes a sellable trust feature. Also derisks the LLM-SaMD precedent set by the FDA clearance today.
**Effort:** 8–10 weeks (1 research engineer + 2 backend engineers)
