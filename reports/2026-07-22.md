**Rationale:** The HuggingFace benchmark ([link](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.18144)) shows LLM-based SBDD is now competitive with diffusion under spatial constraints. **Effort:** Large (10–14 weeks; requires model integration, spatial-constraint DSL, and validation on internal pharma benchmarks).

**Rationale:** [AgentDebugX](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.18754), [CodeRescue](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19338v1), and [Agents in the Wild](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19336v1) collectively signal that agent observability is the current productionization bottleneck. Ship this before customer escalations force it.
**Effort:** Medium (5–7 weeks; instrumentation + replay UI on top of existing agent orchestration).
