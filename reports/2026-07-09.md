## 6. Build Signals for K Platform

### 🥇 Agent trajectory observability + reliability layer
**Rationale:** Three converging signals today (AgentLens, ADE-PRF, Root Cause Extraction) show the field moving from "did the agent succeed?" to "diagnose long-horizon agent health." Pharma will not deploy K agents at GxP scale without trajectory-level auditability. This is also the credible answer to "why not ChatGPT Work?" — validated, monitored, explainable agent runs.
**Effort:** Medium (1 quarter for MVP; leverages [AgentLens](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.06624), [ADE-PRF](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07689v1), and [trace root-cause paper](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07702v1))

**Rationale:** Nature Biotechnology flags digital twins as the emergent category in biotech R&D. K's multimodal (pathology + genomics + clinical) data assets are uniquely suited to virtual-patient simulation for trial optimization. Ship a "K Digital Twin" narrative before Benchling or NVIDIA Clara own it.
**Effort:** Large (2 quarters; requires simulation stack + validation dataset)

### 🥉 Validated deployment-rules framework for multi-agent K workflows
**Rationale:** The Institutional Red-Teaming paper reframes safety around *deployment rules*, not just model weights — a perfect abstraction for GxP-validated agent policies. Combined with the OpenAI Bio Bounty precedent, K should productize configurable, auditable rule-sets that pharma QA can sign off on.
**Effort:** Medium (1 quarter; policy DSL + red-team harness building on [Institutional Red-Teaming](http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07695v1))
