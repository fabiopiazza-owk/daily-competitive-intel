- **In-Context Batch Effect Correction (⭐ Most relevant):** The in-context control samples paper ([arXiv 2604.20824](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20824v1)) proposes including reference control images at inference time to let the model self-normalize across imaging domains. This is a paradigm shift from the current approach of stain normalization or domain adaptation at training time. K's ML team should evaluate this within 2 sprints.

- **DeepFAN: Transformer for Human-AI Collaborative Radiology:** Multi-reader, multi-case trial of a transformer-based model for pulmonary nodule assessment ([PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42020549/)). Methodology for human-AI collaboration trials is increasingly standardized; relevant template for how K Platform should structure validation studies for pathology AI.
